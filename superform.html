    • Peripheral Furniture VR
    A speculative project on simulating future IoT experience in VR.
    VR | Unreal Engine | Environmental/Level Design | Interaction Design | Prototyping

    IoT home speculation: What if our furnitures could also function as peripheral devices to control electronics in the living space?



    What are peripherals?
    Peripherals are devices used to communicate with our electronics. Common peripherals include mice, keyboards, and so on. They are typically interacted by using our fingers (to touch, to press. etc.).

    What are furnitures?
    Furniture are staple objects intended to support various human activities such as seating, eating, and sleeping.

    What if furnitures could also functions as peripherals?
    Smart home sector has been seeing a significant amount of growth in the past years and as we entered the age of 5G, home IoT is only growing faster. However, our furnitures - the most fundamental pieces in a living space - are often neglected when it comes to smart home.

    Objectives of this project
    To simulate a speculative smart home in VR where electronics are controlled through interacting with our furnitures.
    To create a heightened experience of using our whole body to interact with surrounding electronics, as opposed to using our hands and voices.
