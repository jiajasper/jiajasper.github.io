    3-week Furniture Design Solo Project @idecourse
    VR | Unreal Engine | Environmental/Level Design | Interaction Design | Prototyping

    Your furnitures are also your peripherals!


    Imagine your sofa at home could be used to control your room light. Cool, Ay?

    Peripherals are devices used to communicate with our electronics, eg. mice and keyboards.
    Furnitures are staple objects intended to support daily human activities such as seating, eating, and sleeping.

    The smart home sector has been seeing a significant amount of growth as we entered the age of 5G. However, our furnitures are not getting smarter.




    "What if our furnitures could replace our conventional peripheral devices to control electronics in the living space?"

    "Let's simulate this experience in an immersive VR environment!"

    Keycap Shaped VR Stool



    The form of the VR stool draws inspriration from keycaps and the semiotics of this shape invites the users to try to interact with the stool. The keycap shape is created by stretching and tying a piece of lycra over the internals of the stool.
