    Physical Computing | Prototyping | Resin | Mechatronics | Object Detection


    NIKU (ニク) is a clingy and attention-seeking robot companion who tracks down and headbutts human feet non-stop. Through this mildly annoying yet loving interaction, we hope to fill the human psychological need of being wanted in this ever isolating society.

    It has a soft white body made of silicone which resembles a juicy and meaty bun - hence the name NIKU(ニク: meat in Japanese).



    NIKU was a 3-week physical computing project at MSc/MA Innovation Design Engineering (Imperical College and Royal College of Arts). We were prompted to make an object that is playful and interacts with human.
